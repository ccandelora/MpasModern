## Revolutionizing the Way You Serve and Protect

The challenges facing public safety and local government agencies are constantly evolving. Success requires leaders who can adapt quickly while ensuring a solid operational foundation. And that requires a public safety solutions company you can trust.

Our customers choose Lexipol to make an investment in the safety and security of their personnel, their agencies and their communities. Our mission-critical solutions— [state-specific policy development and management](https://www.lexipol.com/solutions/policies-and-updates/), [online trainin](https://www.lexipol.com/solutions/online-learning/) g, [mobile wellness resources](https://www.lexipol.com/solutions/wellness-resources/), [grant services](https://www.lexipol.com/solutions/grant-services/) and [industry news sites](https://www.lexipol.com/solutions/industry-news/)—help agencies improve the quality of life for all community members. When new challenges arise—such as COVID-19, the opioid epidemic or an increase in active-shooter events—we create compliance resources and training to help you keep up. So you can spend more time focusing on operational priorities and less time worrying whether your personnel are prepared.

Lexipol is your partner in preparation for today’s challenges, helping you mitigate risk, bring your personnel home safely at the end of each shift, and save lives within your communities.

And we do it with a team of dedicated individuals with experience in constitutional law, civil rights, ADA and discrimination, mental health, psychology, labor negotiations, Internal Affairs, use of force, hazmat and a whole lot more.
